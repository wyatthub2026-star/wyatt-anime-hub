🌊 Moana

📖 Description

Moana follows a brave young woman who sets out on an incredible journey across the ocean. Along the way, she discovers courage, friendship, and the importance of her connection to her people and the sea.

🎭 Cast

  • Catherine Lagaʻaia as Moana
  • Dwayne Johnson as Maui
  • John Tui as Chief Tui
  • Frankie Adams as Sina
  • Rena Owen as Gramma Tala
